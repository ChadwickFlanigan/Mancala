Mancala!

Welcome to the game guide! This page is where you'll find all of the important information about how to run your Mancala! application.

Menu Options:

At the top left of your application screen, you'll see two menu options: Game and Computer Player.

Game

This drop down menu will reveal the Exit option, a button used to close the application whenever you believe you just can't handle anymore fun!

Computer Player

The Computer Player menu gives you the option to choose the strategy your computer opponent will use: the Near option will make the computer choose the nearest legal pit to it's point pit from which to make a move. The Far option forces the computer to choose the pit furthest from it's point pit. The Random option will ensure that the computer uses no logic to pick a pit. Rather, the computer will obey the skeins of fate - will this be to your advantage, or detriment?
